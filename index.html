About the Studio

A message from Haruka Kikuchi Piano Studio.

Professional • Personal • Passionate

Haruka Kikuchi Piano Studio is led by a professional in music education. I value each student’s individuality, and my mission is to share the beauty of music through the piano. With passion and care, I support students in growing alongside music and experiencing the joy of self-expression.

Lesson Programs

Programs are designed to support steady growth—from fundamentals to advanced artistry and special goals.

Customized learning

Foundation Course

Learn essential piano technique and how to read sheet music. With personalized, one-to-one guidance, you will build a reliable foundation and improve steadily.

  • Posture, hand shape, and healthy technique
  • Reading notes and rhythm with confidence
  • Step-by-step practice methods

Advanced Course

Develop higher-level performance skills and deepen your understanding of music theory. Training focuses on musical interpretation and expressive playing.

  • Advanced technique and tone control
  • Harmony, analysis, and musical structure
  • Expression, phrasing, and artistry

Special Course

A customized program based on your interests and goals. This may include preparing for concerts, auditions, or music competitions—supporting a wide range of activities.

  • Competition and recital preparation
  • Repertoire planning and performance coaching
  • Personal goal setting and pacing

Events

Students can join recitals, concerts, and studio events held regularly. Performing is a great way to celebrate progress and enjoy music with others.

  • Regular studio recitals
  • Concert opportunities
  • A supportive and motivating environment

Recording & Video

We can record audio and video of your performance to keep a personal growth archive. Reviewing recordings helps you evaluate your playing objectively and find clear points to improve.

  • Audio/video recording for progress tracking
  • Self-evaluation and improvement guidance
  • Optional performance portfolio support
