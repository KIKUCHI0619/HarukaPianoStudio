Biography

Haruka Kikuchi

Piano teacher • Wezembeek-Oppem

Short bio

Born in Japan, Haruka Kikuchi graduated from the Music Department of Hyogo Prefectural Nishinomiya High School and later earned a Bachelor’s degree from the School of Music at Kobe College. She is currently enrolled in the first year of the Master’s program at the Royal Conservatory of Brussels.

In 2024, she was awarded 5th Prize in the General Division at the 24th Great Wall Cup International Music Competition. Alongside her performance career, she has extensive teaching experience in Japan, having worked as an instructor at a major music school.
